# 1. Communication

## Formal Communication
- Professional
- Clear
- Concise
- Respectful
- Objective

Example:
> Good morning, could you please review the attached document?

---

## Informal Communication

Used among friends or close teammates.

Example:
> Let's discuss after lunch.

---

## Verbal Communication

- Meetings
- Presentations
- Phone calls
- Interviews

---

## Written Communication

- Emails
- Reports
- Documentation
- Chat messages

---

## Non-verbal Communication

- Eye contact
- Facial expressions
- Posture
- Hand gestures
- Tone of voice

---

# 2. Email Etiquette

## Structure

Subject

Greeting

Body

Closing

Signature

Example

Subject: Leave Request for 18 July

Dear Sir,

I would like to request leave for 18 July due to a medical appointment.

Thank you.

Regards,
John

---

## Avoid

❌ ALL CAPS
❌ Slang
❌ Emojis
❌ Grammar mistakes
❌ Missing subject
