# $\fbox{ECONOMICS}$





## **Topic - 1: Basic Economic Terms**

|Term|Definition / Full Form|AFCAT Favourite Question|
|---|---|---|
|GDP|Gross Domestic Product|Nominal vs Real GDP|
|GNP|Gross National Product|GDP + income from abroad|
|NNP|Net National Product|GNP − depreciation|
|Repo Rate|Rate at which RBI lends to commercial banks|Current rate: 6.5% (2025)|
|Reverse Repo|Rate at which RBI borrows from banks|0.25% below Repo|
|CRR|Cash Reserve Ratio (banks keep with RBI)|Current: 4.5%|
|SLR|Statutory Liquidity Ratio|18% (govt securities)|
|CPI|Consumer Price Index|Measures retail inflation|
|WPI|Wholesale Price Index|Measures wholesale inflation|
|Fiscal Deficit|Govt expenditure − total revenue (excl. borrowings)|2024–25: 4.9% of GDP|
|Revenue Deficit|Revenue expenditure − revenue receipts||
|Primary Deficit|Fiscal deficit − interest payments||
|MGNREGA|Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act|100 days, 40% women|
|PMJDY|Pradhan Mantri Jan Dhan Yojana|Zero-balance accounts|
|PM-KISAN|Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi|₹6000/year to farmers|



## **Topic - 2: RBI Functions & Monetary Tools**

- **Governor (2025):** Shaktikanta Das (6th term)
- **HQ:** Mumbai
- **Monetary Policy Committee:** 6 members (3 RBI, 3 external)
- **Tools:** Repo, Reverse Repo, CRR, SLR, Bank Rate, Open Market Operations
- **Inflation Target:** 4% ± 2% (MPC mandate)
- **Base Rate:** Minimum rate banks can charge customers
- **NEFT/RTGS:** Electronic fund transfers
- **Priority Sector Lending:** 40% of adjusted net bank credit

## **Topic - 5: Economic Indicators**

- **Current GDP Growth (2024–25):** 6.5–7% (IMF projection)
- **Inflation Target:** 4% ± 2%
- **Foreign Exchange Reserves:** ~$650 billion (2025)
- **Unemployment Rate:** ~7–8% (CMIE data)
- **FDI Inflows (2023–24):** $70.9 billion
- **Major Export Items:** Petroleum, gems, pharma, engineering goods
- **Major Import Items:** Crude oil, gold, electronics
- **RBI Repo Rate (Dec 2025):** 6.5% (hold since Feb 2023)
