## **Topic - 3: Most Frequently Asked Questions**

- **Who gave the slogan “Do or Die”?** → Mahatma Gandhi (Quit India)
- **Who was the first Indian woman president of INC?** → Annie Besant (1917) or Sarojini Naidu (1925) – both asked
- **In which session was Purna Swaraj resolution passed?** → Lahore 1929
- **Who founded the Indian Home Rule League?** → Bal Gangadhar Tilak (1916) & Annie Besant (1916)
- **Who led the Bardoli Satyagraha?** → Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel (1928)
- **Who called Gandhi “half-naked fakir”?** → Winston Churchill
- **“Jai Hind” and “Delhi Chalo” slogans given by?** → Subhas Chandra Bose

## **Topic - 7: Acts & Commissions**

- **1909 Morley-Minto Reforms →** Separate electorate for Muslims
- **1919 Montagu-Chelmsford →** Dyarchy in provinces
- **1935 GOI Act →** Provincial autonomy + All-India Federation (never implemented)
- **1946 Cabinet Mission →** Rejected Pakistan, proposed 3-tier federation
