## **Topic - 4: President Facts**

- Elected for **5 years**
- Maximum **2 terms** (no bar in Constitution, convention)
- Impeachment → **Art 61** (2/3 majority in both houses)
- Election → **Art 54** (MPs + MLAs proportional representation)
- Qualification → 35 years + Lok Sabha qualification
- Pardoning powers → **Art 72**
- Ordinance → **Art 123** (6 months + 6 weeks)
- Emergency → National (352), State (356), Financial (360)
- First President → Dr Rajendra Prasad (2 terms)
- Current (2025) → Droupadi Murmu (15th)
- Vice-President is ex-officio Chairman of Rajya Sabha
- VP elected by MPs of both houses



## **Topic - 5: Parliament & Sessions**

- Maximum gap between sessions → **6 months**
- Minimum sessions per year → **2** (Budget + Monsoon usually 3)
- Quorum → 1/10th of total members
- Joint sitting → **Art 108** (Lok Sabha speaker presides)
