# $\fbox{COGNITIVE FUNCTIONS}$


## 1. Introverted Thinking (Ti)

- Internal consistency
- Definitions & classifications
- Logical relationships between concepts
- Formal structure
- System coherence

>*An information element that evaluates internal structural consistency and logical relationships between concepts.*


## 2. Extraverted Thinking (Te)

- Empirical efficiency
- Measurable results
- Productivity
- External data
- Practical optimization

>*An information element that evaluates external efficiency, measurable results, and practical effectiveness.*


## 3. Introverted Feeling (Fi)

- Personal values
- Loyalty
- Psychological distance
- Moral alignment
- Depth of bonds

>*An information element that evaluates personal value alignment and the quality of interpersonal relationships.*


## 4. Extroverted Feeling (Fe)

- Emotional expression
- Mood regulation
- Group affect
- Visible emotional energy

>*An information element that evaluates and regulates expressed emotional atmosphere within a social context.*


## 5. Introverted Intuition (Ni)

- Temporal flow
- Inevitable outcomes
- Forecasting
- Convergence
- Pattern convergence

>*An information element that evaluates temporal patterns, convergence of events, and probable long-term outcomes.*


## 6. Extraverted Intuition (Ne)

- Alternative options
- Idea generation
- Expansion
- Potential states

>*An information element that evaluates latent possibilities, alternative interpretations, and unrealized potential.*


## 7. Introverted Sensing (Si)

- Comfort
- Physical equilibrium
- Subtle bodily awareness
- Environmental harmony

>*An information element that evaluates internal physical states and environmental equilibrium.*


## 8. Extraverted Sensing (Se)

- Direct impact
- Pressure
- Territory
- Assertive action
- Resource control

>*An information element that evaluates force dynamics, control of space, and direct impact on the external environment.*
