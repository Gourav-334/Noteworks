# $\fbox{Chapter 2: AVERAGE}$





## **Topic - 1: Repeated Questions**

| Sno. | Type                                | Formula / Trick                           | Example                                                                                                       |
| :--: | ----------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  1   | Simple Average                      | Sum of items ÷ Number of items            | Avg of $20, 30, 40 = 90 ÷ 3 = 30$                                                                             |
|  2   | Weighted Average                    | $(n_1x_1 + n_2x_2 + …) ÷ (n_1 + n_2 + …)$ | 5 boys avg 40 kg, 10 girls avg 35 kg → (5×40 + 10×35) ÷ 15 = 550 ÷ 15 = 36.67 kg                              |
|  3   | Average of first $n$ numbers        | $\frac{n(n+1)}{2} ÷ n = \frac{(n+1)}{2}$  | Average of first 20 natural numbers $= \frac{21}{2} = 10.5$                                                   |
|  4   | Average Speed (two equal distances) | $\frac{2ab}{(a+b)}$ (harmonic mean)       | $40\;km/h$ one way, $60\;km/h$ return → $\frac{2 \times 40 \times 60}{(40+60)} = \frac{4800}{100} = 48\;km/h$ |
|  5   | Average after adding/removing       | New avg = Old avg ± (change ÷ $n$)        | Average of $10$ numbers = $50$, add $70$ → new avg = $50 + \frac{20}{10} = 52$                                |
|  6   | Average age after $t$ years         | Present avg + $t$                         | Present average age = $30$ → after $5$ yrs = $35$                                                             |
