## **Topic - 1: Repeated Patterns**

| Shape     | Area                            | Perimeter / Circumference | Volume (3D)            | Lateral / Curved Surface       | Total Surface Area |
| --------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------------ | ------------------ |
| Rectangle | $l \times b$                    | $2(l + b)$                | —                      | —                              | —                  |
| Square    | $a^2$                           | $4a$                      | —                      | —                              | —                  |
| Triangle  | $\frac{1}{2} \times b \times h$ | Sum of sides              | —                      | —                              | —                  |
| Circle    | $\pi r^2$                       | $2 \pi r$                 | —                      | —                              | —                  |
| Cube      | —                               | $12a$                     | $a^3$                  | $4a^2$                         | $6a^2$             |
| Cuboid    | —                               | $4(l + b + h)$            | $l \times b \times h$  | $2h(l + b)$                    | $2(lb + bh + lh)$  |
| Cylinder  | —                               | —                         | $\pi r^2h$             | $2 \pi rh$                     | $2 \pi r(r + h)$   |
| Cone      | —                               | —                         | $\frac{1}{3} \pi r^2h$ | $\pi rl (l' = \sqrt(r^2+h^2))$ | $\pi r(l + r)$     |



## **Topic - 2: Shortcuts / Tricks**

| Situation                     | Shortcut                                                         |
| ----------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Square from diagonal          | Side = $\frac{d}{\sqrt 2}$, Area = $\frac{d^2}{2}$               |
| Cube from diagonal            | Diagonal = $a \sqrt 3$                                           |
| Cuboid from diagonal          | $d = \sqrt(l^2 + b^2 + h^2)$                                     |
| Cone slant height             | $l = \sqrt(r^2 + h^2)$                                           |
| Cylinder volume               | $\pi r²h$ (memorize $\frac{22}{7} \times r^2$ values)            |
| Sphere (rare)                 | Volume =  $\frac{4}{3} πr^3$, S.A. = $4 \pi r^2$                 |
| Change in dimension           | $Area \times (scale)^2$, $Volume × (scale)^3$                    |
| Equilateral triangle area     | $(\frac{\sqrt 3}{4}) a^2$                                        |
| Circle from diameter          | $r = \frac{d}{2}$                                                |
| Perimeter rectangle from area | If $l = kb$, $Area = kb^2$ $\rightarrow$ $Perimeter = 2b(k + 1)$ |
