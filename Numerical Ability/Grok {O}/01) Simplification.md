## **Topic - 1: VBODMAS

| Sno. | Rule          | What to do first (BODMAS/VBODMAS)    | Example (solve in 10 sec)                      |
| ---- | ------------- | ------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- |
| 1    | V – Vinculum  | Solve inside bar first               | $12 + 8 ÷ (4 − 2) = 12 + 8 ÷ 2 = 12 + 4 = 16$  |
| 2    | B – Brackets  | $() \rightarrow \{\} \rightarrow []$ | $(15 − 3 × 4) ÷ 3 = (15 − 12) ÷ 3 = 3 ÷ 3 = 1$ |
| 3    | O – Of        | Treat as multiplication              | $\frac{1}{2}\;of\;24 = 12$                     |
| 4    | D – Division  | Left to right                        | $48 ÷ 8 × 3 = 6 × 3 = 18$                      |
| 5    | M – Mult      | Left to right                        | $5 + 4 × 6 − 2 = 5 + 24 − 2 = 27$              |
| 6    | A/S – Add/Sub | Left to right                        | $20 − 5 + 3 = 18$                              |



## **Topic - 2: Shortcut / Trick**

| Type              | Shortcut Formula / Trick   | Example                                         |
| ----------------- | -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| $a^2 − b^2$       | $(a − b)(a + b)$           | $49² − 1² = (50 − 1)(50 + 1) = 49 × 51 = 2499$  |
| $(a + b)^2$       | $a^2 + b^2 + 2ab$          | $25² = 625$ (memorise 11–30 squares)            |
| $Successive \;\%$ | $x + y + (\frac{xy}{100})$ | $10\%\uparrow\;then\;20\%\uparrow\;=10+20+2=32$ |
| $a^3 − b^3$       | $(a − b)(a^2 + ab + b^2)$  | $5^3 − 3^3 = (5−3)(25+15+9) = 2\times49 = 98$   |
