# $\fbox{Chapter 9: ALGEBRA}$





## **Topic - 1: Repeated Pattern**

| Type                             | Formula / Trick                                                    | Example                                                         |
| -------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Linear Equations (1–2 variables) | $ax + b = c$<br>$x = \frac{c − b}{a}$                              | $2x + 5 = 15$<br>$x = 5$                                        |
| Quadratic Equations              | $ax^2 + bx + c = 0$<br>$x = \frac{[−b ± \sqrt(b^2−4ac)]}{2a}$      | $x^2 − 5x + 6 = 0$<br>$(x−2)(x−3) = 0$<br>$x = 2,3$             |
| Roots nature                     | $D = b^2 − 4ac >0$ real distinct,<br>$D=0$ equal,<br>$D<0$ no real | $D = 25 − 24 = 1 >0 \rightarrow \text{ Two real}$               |
| Sum & Product of roots           | $Sum = - \frac{b}{a}$<br>$Product = \frac{c}{a}$                   | $x² − 7x + 12 = 0$<br>$\rightarrow Sum=7, product=12$           |
| Ratio of roots or numbers        | If roots $m:n$<br>Equation $x^2 − (m+n)x + mn = 0$                 | Roots $2:3$<br>$x^2 − 5x + 6 = 0$                               |
| Age / Number problems            | Present ratio, after $t$ yrs ratio                                 | Present $3:5$, after $5$ yrs $4:6 \rightarrow$ ages $15$ & $25$ |



## **Topic - 2: Shortcuts / Tricks**

| Situation                         | Shortcut                                                                 |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Quadratic factors                 | Find two numbers sum = $b$, product = $c$                                |
| $D = 0$                           | $\text{Roots equal} = \frac{b}{2a}$                                      |
| New equation with roots $k$ times | Substitute $\frac{x}{k}$                                                 |
| Roots $m$, $n$                    | Equation $(x−m)(x−n) = 0$                                                |
| Age problems                      | Let present ages $kx$, $ky \rightarrow$ after $t$ yrs $(kx+t):(ky+t)$    |
| Cross multiply ratios             | $kx + t = m(ky + t)$                                                     |
| Sum & product given               | Directly write $x^2 − (Sum)x + Product = 0$                              |
| Ratio of roots                    | Assume roots $kr$, $ks \rightarrow$ equation $x^2 − k(r+s)x + k^2rs = 0$ |
| $D$ calculation                   | Memorize perfect squares up to $30² = 900$                               |
| Simple linear                     | Transfer terms                                                           |
