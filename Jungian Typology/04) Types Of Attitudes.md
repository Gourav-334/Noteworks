## I. Introversion

*Introversion* is one of the two attitudes of the psyche. This attitude orients around the *subjective factor* to process the contents of consciousness. So what is a *subjective factor*? Subjective factor here refers to the personal standpoint of a subject. This personal standpoint is decided as per what *impression* their ego possesses. Impression in simple terms can be understood as a very systematic and internally determinant ideas.
